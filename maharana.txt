1576 ka samay tha. Mewar ka raja Maharana Pratap tha, jo kisi bhi haalat me Akbar ki baadshahat sweekar nahi kar raha tha. Akbar ne Mewar ko jhukane ke liye kai baar sandhi bheji, par Pratap ne har baar thukra diya. Tab Akbar ne yudh ka faisla liya. Usne apne sabse vishwasniya senapati Man Singh ko 80,000 se adhik sena ke saath bheja — jisme Rajput, Pathan, Afgaan, aur Mughal sainik the.
Maharana Pratap ko pata tha ki mukabla seedha nahi hoga, kyunki unke paas kul mila kar 20,000 ke aaspaas sainik hi the. Lekin unhone kabhi sankhya nahi dekhi, unhone dekha sirf apna dharm, apni mitti, aur apna swabhimaan. Ve samjhte the ki jung ho ya jang, jeet hamesha hausle se hoti hai.
18 June 1576 ko subah Haldi Ghati ki ghatiyon me yudh ki ghoshna hui. Ghati ekdum sankri thi — dono taraf pahaad, beech me ek patli si zameen. Maharana ne yahi faida uthaya. Unhone apne sainikon ko teen hisso me baant diya – mukhya sena, paschimi pahadiyon me chhipi gupt sena, aur ek chalak sena jo samay par peechhe se vaar kare.
Jang ki shuruaat Maharana Pratap ne apne favorite ghode Chetak par sawar ho kar ki. Chetak ne toofan ki tarah Mughalon ke rankon me ghus kar tabahi macha di. Maharana ne khud Man Singh ke haathi par prahar kiya, haathi ghabra gaya, Man Singh bach gaya, lekin uski himmat toot gayi. Mughalon ka morcha hil gaya.
Wahiin dusri taraf pahadiyon se Shakti Singh ki sena ne gupt hamla kiya aur Mughal piche hatne lage. Mughalon ki peeth ki taraf se bhi rajputon ne ghera daal diya. Mughal sena behaal ho gayi.
Yudh kuch ghanto tak chala, khoon ki nadiyaan bahein, par aakhir me Mughalon ne jab dekha ki ve phas gaye hain, unhone yudh bhoomi chhod di. Man Singh bhag gaya, Mughal sainik bhaag khade hue, aur Mewar ke veeron ka vijay nishan Haldi Ghati ki mitti par lahrane laga.
Yeh sach hai ki Pratap ne turant Mewar wapas nahi jeeta, par Mughalon ko yeh samaj aa gaya ki Maharana ko haraana asambhav hai. Akbar itna hila ki usne kabhi is yudh ka jashn nahi manaya, kyunki andar hi andar samaj gaya tha — Mughal jeete nahi, haar gaye the.
Haldi Ghati ka yudh ek din ka tha, lekin uska prabhav sadiyon tak raha. Ye sirf talwaron ka yudh nahi tha, ye swabhimaan, swatantrata aur rajputi shaurya ka pratyaksh pramaan tha.
Maharana Pratap ne baad me 1582 me Dewair me Mughalon ki 36 chowkiyaan tod kar pramaan de diya ki Haldi Ghati ke baad unka josh kabhi nahi toota. Ve jeete the, ve ant tak jeete rahe.
Haldi Ghati ka yudh asli arth me veerata ka prateek hai — jahan sankhya nahi, sankalp jeeta. Jahan rajgaddi nahi, mitti ki raksha jeeti. Aur jahan ek raja nahi, ek yug ka itihas jeeta.
